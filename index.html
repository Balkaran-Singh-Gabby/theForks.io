Hello Winnipeg!

Welcome to TheForks.io

Winnipeg is home to a robust tech scene that is beginning to evolve in new and exciting ways.

This space is for programmers of all experience levels to share and collaborate!

The Forks historical site embodies a spirit of teamwork and communication.

theForks.io is for Manitoban programmers, developers, and designers to share, discover, and discuss local open-source programs and projects.

Whether you've built a handy CLI tool, a small web app, or just a fun experiment, we want to showcase it here!

  • Community Participation
  • Networking
