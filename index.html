Winnipeg's Open-Source Repository

Hello Winnipeg! Welcome to TheForks.io

Winnipeg is home to a robust tech scene that is beginning to evolve in new and exciting ways. The Forks historical site embodies a spirit of teamwork and communication. TheForks.io is for Manitoban programmers, developers, and designers to share, discover, and discuss local open-source programs and projects. Whether you've built a handy CLI tool, a small web app, or just a fun experiment, we want to showcase it here!

This space is for programmers of all experience levels.

This is a ground level project. Where this space goes and how it evolves will be up to the community. Have any ideas on how to use this space? Want to do more than just contribute?

Why Contribute?

  • Visibility
  • Community Participation
  • Networking
